# Introduction

Welcome to "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: A Complete Course for Embodied Intelligence"! This textbook serves as your definitive guide to understanding and building intelligent robotic systems that interact with the physical world.

This comprehensive course covers the foundational theories and practical applications necessary to design, develop, and deploy humanoid robots. From the core principles of artificial intelligence and robotics to advanced simulation techniques and vision-language-action systems, you will gain the expertise to navigate the complex landscape of embodied intelligence.

Prepare to embark on a journey that will equip you with the skills to shape the future of robotics and AI.
